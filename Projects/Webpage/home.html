Executive Summary

Light Savers is a 2 player 3D top-down game in the horror genre, set in an abandoned research facility. The game has four shadow monster enemies, each with differing strenghts and skills that spawn in the shadows. The players cast light from their torches, creating areas of light that monsters cannot spawn in. This is the core game mechanic, and encourages players to stick together and move quickly

Story Line

Set far in the future, Light Savers takes place on an abandoned planet in an alien research facility. A squad of space marines, the players, crash land near the facility. They must find their way to the transmatter teleporter room inside to get back to the mothership. Upon entering the facility they find it shrouded in darkness and infested with an aggressive alien species that attack from the shadows. The players cast light around them, using their torches and light up the levels as they clear the facility of the infestation to get to their destination.

Platform

Light Savers was developed in C# and XNA 3.1. It makes use of the LightPrePass library for it's lighting and shadow-casting features.Light Savers can run on the XBox360 and Windows PC.

Features

3D environment
Light Savers makes use of 3D cameras and 3D assets that move through the world. All players are kept inside the camera's view frustrum by zooming the camera in and out and panning the camera along the level.

Visual effects
Player characters' head torches give off light that penetrates the darkness of the environment. Point light sources are used to enhance and highlight certain features of the environment. The core mechanic of the game is that monsters spawn in dark areas.

Consistent theme
Light Savers' game genre is action-horror. To link in with this, our theme is dark horror and our world will be rendered in a low-polygon style with eerie ambient music and sound effects.
