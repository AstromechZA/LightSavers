Story Line

Set far in the future, Light Savers takes place in a research facility that has been abandoned for many years due to alien medical experiments that went out of control. A squad of space marines, the players, crash land when attempting to investigate the facility and must find their way to the transmatter teleporter room where lies their only means of escape. Upon entering the facility they find it shrouded in darkness and infested with aggressive alien species that attack from the shadows. The players bring light with them as they clear the facility of the infestation.

Summary. It'll blow your mind.

Light Savers is a 2 player 3D side-scroller of the horror-action genre. The game is set in a futuristic world, after a marine spacecraft crashes on an abandoned planet, inhabited by shadow monsters. The players must work together, to fight against these monsters and make it to the old military base station to teleport back to the mothership.
