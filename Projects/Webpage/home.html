Story Line

Set far in the future, Light Savers takes place on an abandoned planet in an alien research facility. A squad of space marines, the players, crash land when attempting to investigate the planet. They must then find their way to the transmatter teleporter room within the fascility to get back to the mothership. Upon entering the facility they find it shrouded in darkness and infested with aggressive alien species that attack from the shadows. The players bring light with them as they clear the facility of the infestation to get to their destination.

Summary. It'll blow your mind.

Light Savers is a 2 player 3D top-down horror shooter. It is set in a futuristic world after a spacecraft crashes on an abandoned planet, inhabited by shadow monsters. The players must work together to fight against these monsters and make it back to the mothership.
